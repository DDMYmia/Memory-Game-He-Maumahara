He Maumahara
In this classic memory game, match all pairs of cards in the quickest time possible.
Hover over the scrollbar to reveal Level 1
The order of the cards for Level 1 never change.

How to Play
To select a card, click it with the left mouse button. Once flipped, choose a second card to make a pair. If the two cards match they will disappear from the board; if they do not match, they will be hidden.


You can use the Show Cards button to reveal every card on the board - and Hide Cards button to cover them again - located in the same place - under the menu button. Cards can be selected while they are hidden, not shown, so use the button wisely.
